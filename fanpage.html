Bio

Stevie Nicks is an American singer and song writer known for her distinct voice and whimsical presence on stage. She also was apart of the popular group Fleetwood Mac. She joined the band with her early partner, Lindsey Buckingham. She later pursued a solo career in 1981. Her career totalled over 40 top 50 hits and sold over 140 million records.
